WATERMELON


From Cucurbitaceous family


More than 1,000 varieties

Where does it grow?

It originally came from South Africa and is now grown in favourable climates from tropical to temperate regions worldwide.


Features:

  • Sweet
  • Juicy flesh
  • Usually Deep red to pink inside
  • Has many black seeds
  • Some seedless varieties exist
  • 91% Water
  • 6% Sugars
